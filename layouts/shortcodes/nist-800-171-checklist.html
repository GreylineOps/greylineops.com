SPRS Submission Summary
Scoring methodology: Each control is worth 1, 3, or 5 points. Not meeting a control deducts its full value from 110. Partial implementation counts as not met under the DoD methodology — create a POA&M for all unmet items. Submit via PIEE at sprs.csd.disa.mil or by email to webptsmh@navy.mil. Scores must be refreshed every 3 years. Inaccurate scores may trigger False Claims Act liability.
